Sky Dive for the Rowans Hospice!

7 May 2016

Netheravon Airfield, Salisbury, SP4 9RY

Fly like a bird!

A breathtaking 2.5 mile high parachute jump: jumping 13,000 ft in tandem with an instructor, you will freefall 5,000ft reaching 120mph and then as your parachute opens you will experience a tranquil ride back to the drop zone!

Divers must be 16 years and over and under 16 stone.

Cost of Registration £225, which includes training and equipment, you are asked to raise a minimum sponsorship of £200 making £425 in total.

The event can also be arranged for any day to suit you.

For more information, call 023 9223 8532 or email fundraising@rowanshospice.co.uk
