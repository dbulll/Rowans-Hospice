Car Raffle

31 March 2016

Win a brand new Citroen C1, or £5,000 cash, in The Rowans Hospice Car Raffle thanks to the generosity of the Richmond Motor Group, who again have donated a car.

Tickets are available for just £1 each from The Rowans Hospice reception or from any Rowans shop. A C1 will be around and about within our area of care to promote the car raffle so look out for it in local shopping centres and supermarkets. Follow us on Facebook to keep up-to-date.

The draw for this amazing prize will take place on 31st March 2016.

For more information or to request books of tickets, please call 023 9223 7149.
