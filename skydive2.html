Sky Dive on a day of your choice!

Netheravon Airfield, Salisbury, SP4 9RY

Fly like a bird! On a day to suit YOU!

Help us raise funds by making an exhilarating 10,000 feet freefall tandem parachute jump!

Imagine, freefalling for 5,000 feet reaching speeds of 120 mph before opening your parachute for a 5 minute canopy ride taking in the stunning landscape before landing in front of spectators with friends and family having a grandstand view!

Divers must be 16 years and over and under 16 stone.

Cost of Registration £225, which includes training and equipment, you are asked to raise a minimum sponsorship of £200 making £425 in total.

For more information, call 023 9223 8532 or email fundraising@rowanshospice.co.uk
