Moonlit Memories Walk

  • 18 June 2016 - around Southsea, Portsmouth, starting from Castle Field, Southsea
  • for more information phone 023 9223 8533 or email moonlit-memories@rowanshospice.co.uk
  • Our annual 6-mile walk or a 12-mile challenge!
  • Join us for our Moonlit Memories Walk under a star-lit sky along Southsea Seafront, through Old Portsmouth and the Historic Dockyard where HMS Victory will be illuminated.
  • There is also an opportunity to light a candle in memory of a loved one in Portsmouth Anglican Cathedral. Watch this space for registration details.
